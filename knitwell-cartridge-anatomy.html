Order Status & Tracking

When a customer asks about an order, follow these steps before sharing any details:

Step 1: Verify

Confirm the order number and the email or ZIP on file before disclosing any order details.

Step 2: Look Up

Use the lookup_order action to retrieve current status and tracking.

Step 3: Share & Anticipate

Share tracking and the estimated delivery date. Proactively offer help with sizing or a free exchange in case the fit isn't perfect.
